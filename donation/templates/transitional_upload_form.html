Temporary page to upload donations from drupal

Do the following:

  1. Login in to the main site
  2. Go to https://effectivealtruism.org.au/donate#overlay-context=donate
  3. Click Results -> Download
  4. Delimited text -> Comma (,)
  5. Click on "Label" once (won't appear to do anything, that's okay)
  6. Click on "Field key" once (won't appear to do anything, that's okay)
  7. Click "Download" at the bottom. It doesn't matter what the file name is.
  8. Upload the file below
